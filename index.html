Hormuz Navigator

Navigate an oil tanker through the Strait of Hormuz!

Phase 1 — Minesweeper
Tap/click to reveal cells. Long-press/right-click to flag.
Clear a path from the left edge to the right edge.

Phase 2 — Transit
Your ship sails through the cleared path.
Use ↑/↓ to speed up or reverse. Space to stop.
Click FPV drones to shoot them down (shotgun)!
Bigger ships have a gunner — click shaheds to destroy them (auto cannon).
Dodge missiles by changing speed — no defense against those.
One hit = game over. Bigger ships = more cargo but more threats.
